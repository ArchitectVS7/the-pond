# Workflow Guides

Complete guides for each Thursian workflow phase.

---

## Available Guides

### Phase 1: Ideation
**File:** [01-ideation-guide.md](./01-ideation-guide.md)

Transform raw ideas into exciting, feasible visions through 8-round dialectic conversation between Dreamer and Doer.

**Key Topics:**
- Overview and purpose
- Input/output specifications
- Round-by-round breakdown
- Agent descriptions
- Manual execution steps
- Success criteria

---

### Phase 2: Focus Group
**File:** [02-focus-group-guide.md](./02-focus-group-guide.md)

Validate vision with domain-specific user personas backed by parallel real-world research.

**Key Topics:**
- Dynamic persona generation
- Parallel web research
- Reality validation
- 5 discussion rounds
- Research alignment
- Focus group synthesis

---

### Phase 3: Engineering Meeting
**File:** [03-engineering-meeting-guide.md](./03-engineering-meeting-guide.md)

Transform validated vision into comprehensive PRD with technical research and expert collaboration.

**Key Topics:**
- Domain-specific engineering personas
- Parallel technical research
- 5 meeting rounds (feasibility, landscape, requirements, dependencies, success)
- Research alignment per round
- PRD synthesis
- Handoff to stakeholder review

---

### Phase 4: Stakeholder Review
**File:** [04-stakeholder-review-guide.md](./04-stakeholder-review-guide.md)

Validate PRD with original ideation team and focus group participants through structured feedback.

**Key Topics:**
- 7 PRD sections reviewed
- 5 feedback rounds per section
- Prioritization (critical vs nice-to-have)
- Stakeholder consensus
- Final approval decision
- PRD revision or approval

---

## Workflow Sequence

```
01-ideation-flow.yaml
    ↓ (Vision Document)
02-focus-group-flow.yaml
    ↓ (Focus Group Report)
03-engineering-meeting-flow.yaml
    ↓ (PRD v1)
04-stakeholder-review-flow.yaml
    ↓ (Approved PRD)
05-technical-design-flow.yaml (planned)
```

---

## Quick Reference

| Phase | Duration | Agents | Outputs |
|-------|----------|--------|---------|
| Ideation | 20-30 min | 3 | Vision document |
| Focus Group | 30-45 min | 8 | Focus group report + research |
| Engineering | 45-60 min | 7 | PRD + technical research |
| Stakeholder | 60-90 min | 10 | Approved PRD + feedback |
