## Round-by-Round Summary

### Round 1: Foundation
**Dreamer**: Pushed for dynamic environmental hazards, rival frog factions, boss frogs as ecological disasters, conspiracy meta-narrative
**Doer**: Grounded it into Vampire Survivors meets Nuclear Throne, defined 3-tier mutation system, 15-20 min runs, meta-progression via conspiracy board
**Convergence**: Core loop established - combat + mutations + conspiracy investigation

### Round 2: Refinement
**Dreamer**: Mutation synergies (Binding of Isaac style), interactive conspiracy board, environmental reactivity, trade-off mutations, The Pond as final boss
**Doer**: Scoped to 30 base mutations with 20 synergies, conspiracy board as Obra Dinn-style investigation, tiered environmental effects, 3 bosses + pond final boss
**Convergence**: Systems have depth without overwhelming scope

### Round 3: User Experience
**Dreamer**: Emphasized JUICE (screen shake, particles), dynamic audio evolution, narrative breadcrumbs via radio transmissions, character personality, accessibility via difficulty zones, moral ambiguity
**Doer**: Systematized juice as polish phase, defined audio crossfade system, character animation requirements, difficulty zones as numeric tweaks, moral consequence via pollution index
**Convergence**: The game will FEEL amazing and make players complicit

### Round 4: Alternatives & Edge Cases
**Dreamer**: Proposed multiple frog species, co-op mode, multiple endings, user-generated conspiracies, weather effects, modding support
**Doer**: Kept 3 endings + weather for MVP, deferred species/co-op/modding to post-launch, proposed starter loadouts as compromise
**Convergence**: Smart scoping - keep what matters, defer what's extra

### Round 5: Scaling & Future Vision
**Dreamer**: Post-launch expansions (Saltwater, Urban Sprawl), seasonal events, speedrun mode, PvP, board game, animated series, REAL activism integration (10% to conservation)
**Doer**: Validated activism angle as brilliant marketing, created realistic post-launch roadmap, defined investor pitch structure, projected $280K Year 1 revenue
**Convergence**: This can be commercially successful AND meaningful

### Round 6: Phased Rollout & Timeline
**Dreamer**: Proposed Early Access strategy (Hades/Risk of Rain 2 model), ARG marketing, streamer seeding, modding from day one, building a PLATFORM not just a game
**Doer**: Formalized 12-month development roadmap (6 months to Early Access, 12 to 1.0), defined budget ($18K-$78K), created marketing strategy, validated modding timeline
**Convergence**: Clear path from prototype to launch to post-support

## Key Innovations Discovered

1. **Satire AS Gameplay**: Not a message ON TOP of mechanics, but EMBEDDED in them
2. **Emotional Arc**: Transforms "fun frog game" into meaningful experience through 3-act structure
3. **Pollution Index**: Persistent world state that makes players confront their complicity
4. **Conspiracy Board**: Investigation becomes meta-progression (Obra Dinn meets roguelike)
5. **True Ending**: Protest mechanic teaches systemic change > individual violence
6. **Activism Integration**: 10% to conservation orgs, real-world impact tracking
