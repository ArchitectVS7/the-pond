## âœ¨ Key Features

### Core Gameplay Loop
- **Wave-Based Combat**: 15-20 minute runs with escalating enemy swarms every 60 seconds
- **Mutation System**: 30 base mutations across 5 categories (Tongue, Mobility, Elemental, Defense, Utility)
- **Synergy Discoveries**: 20+ hard-coded combinations that create emergent gameplay (Sticky Tongue + Electric = Tesla Lasso)
- **Resource Collection**: Microplastics (common), toxins (rare), alchemical components (boss drops)

### The Conspiracy Board
- **Investigation-as-Progression**: Between runs, piece together evidence on an interactive board
- **Multiple Solutions**: Connect clues to uncover 3 distinct conspiracy endings (Corporate, Government, Nihilist)
- **Secret True Ending**: Complete all 3 paths to unlock a protest-based final sequence where you organize instead of fight

### Environmental Storytelling
- **Dynamic Hazards**: Water level shifts, pollution pools, weather effects (acid rain, drought, fog)
- **Visual Reactivity**: Your mutation build changes your appearance and environment (toxic build = green aura)
- **Persistent World State**: Pollution index tracks YOUR contribution across runsâ€”high pollution = harder future runs
- **Ecological Boss Fights**: Each boss personifies an environmental disaster (Oil Spill Bullfrog, Pesticide Dart Frog, Mercury Runoff Toad)

### Progression & Replayability
- **3 Starter Loadouts**: Brawler (melee), Ranger (ranged), Scientist (experimental)
- **Trade-Off Mutations**: High-risk/high-reward cursed mutations with drawbacks
- **Daily Challenges**: Seeded runs with leaderboards
- **Meta-Unlocks**: New mutations, frog skins, and challenge modes via conspiracy discoveries

### The "Juice" (Polish That Matters)
- **Screen Shake & Particle Effects**: Every hit feels CRUNCHY (Vlambeer's "Art of Screenshake")
- **Dynamic Audio**: Soundtrack evolves from peaceful pond ambience to glitchy industrial chaos as pollution intensifies
- **Character Personality**: Expressive frog animations (victory hops, disgust faces, idle croaks)
- **Radio Transmissions**: Between waves, intercept corporate memos and warnings that build the world
